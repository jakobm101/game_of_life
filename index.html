Protosoph

Deciphering complex theory through localization and information enrichment

About Protosoph

Protosoph is a print medium that is conceived as the missing first chapter to complex texts, exploring Adorno's masterminds. Its premiere release tackles the intellectual titan, Karl Marx, and his opus magnum, "Das Kapital". The initial pages of this treatise present a formidable challenge with their intricate phrasing and profound vocabulary, hewn from Marx's rigorous studies of Hegelian philosophy and history. Indeed, some concepts may elude the grasp of the uninitiated. Hence, the inaugural issue of Protosoph features the entire chapter on Commodity Fetishism from "Capital", a veritable delight to peruse. Succinct elucidations of pivotal terminologies serve to explicate the text even further. In addition, a modernized interpretation of Marx's work stands in concert with the primary text. The ample pages of a newspaper are a perfect receptacle for these ancillary components, ensuring ample space for the main text and its annotations to unfold.

Percept

A bi-weekly newspaper linking current events to societal issues such as income inequality and climate developments, to enhance reader comprehension.

About Percept

a newspaper that goes beyond the surface level of the latest news to explore the deeper connections that lie beneath. I conceptualized, designed, and created the editorial graphics for this publication, drawing on my expertise in visual communication to create a stunning visual experience for readers. To visually convey the depth of the articles, I focused on the typography, using refined and elegant fonts that communicate the seriousness of the content. Candid photos and illustrations bring the text to life, making it more engaging and encouraging readers to dive deeper into the material. My design philosophy emphasizes clear contrasts between elements, with generous use of blank space to give each page a sense of breathing room. To ensure a cohesive look throughout the publication, I employed a sophisticated grid that underlies the composition of every page. The goal is using the power of design to communicate complex ideas in an engaging and accessible way.
